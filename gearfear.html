Gear Fear

The player controls the cursor using a mouse in order to click and zap gears that are flying towards the Travis robot. The score is calculated based on accuracy and number of gears shot.

Ludum Dare Game Jam

Organisation of Ludum Dare 32 on the Plymouth University campus was done by me. Any time I had spare went onto making this quick game.

How it was done.

Changing up the thinking pattern a little, by starting with a piece of music by Kevin MacLeod, a game was formed around that premise of it sounding like a very mechanical factory. Just going with that this game into fruition.
