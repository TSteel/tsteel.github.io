UPPTS Pokemon Team Generator

Originally designed for internal usage in the Plymouth University Pokemon Trainers Society, this tool was created as a side project, as such it contains ideas and images that I do not own. It features a series of Pokemon that can be randomised for team ideas to be used along side the games.

Not for profit

This is merely a demonstration of skillset. This project is not for any monetary gain.
