_Spider.Game

Featured project for the end of our first year of uni. Made using Unity3D, it started out just playing around with various features of Unity during one of the practical sessions.

What do you do?

You run around 'hitting' a spider whilst it's young try to demolish you. Sounds fun? Yet horrifying... It's playable, to an extent. I based the general gameplay off an earlier success in the form of Poke the Owl, using that clicking mentality, you can try it below!
