Stolen Tea

This was literally the first game I ever made. In hindsight, sure, it's not amazing, but it was created off, literally nothing. Only background knowledge from using computers, but that's about it.

Why?

The basis for this game was working off of Stencyl's collision. Being, you move to drop down, and then let go of the keys to stay still. Originally this was supposed to be a stealth based game, hence a lot of my time went into drawing the character, and getting it to fade out, but that feature was tweaked as the goal changed.

Aim

The aim of the game is to survive as long as possible, the difficulty changing how fast the panels move up the screen. I'm not too keen on how I left the text looking, but lesson learned for the future!
