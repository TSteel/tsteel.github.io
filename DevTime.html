Unity3D game in, based on the theme of Duality. Taking this in the direction of a two player co-operative game, where two players must use a keyboard and XBOX controller to work together collecting and depositing 'Choco' dolls. One player controls launch time and direction, whilst the other decides the launch angle and power.

First Year

Stolen Tea

This was the first game I ever created. It was the first assignment, the only requirement was to use Stencyl. This game focuses on avoiding being pushed up too high or dropping down too low. Simply fall by moving side to side. Reverse of the 'norm', by not pressing any buttons the player remains on the current platform. Initially utilising an old name 'TSteel' a game about tea was created.

Poke the Owl

Soon after the first project was over, I wanted to try making more games. Set a time limit of a few hours and said, "just make a game". Owls are great. Who wouldn't want to prod one? Monotonous, never changing action. It actually became my greatest success of the year. *sigh*. Nothing makes any sense. Regardless, the game itself involves the player clicking on a picture of an owl drawn in a rush using Paint.Net and it sings back.
