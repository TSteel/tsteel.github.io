Ruin Remedy

Take control of Bri, a young lady revisiting her parent's workplace at a local hospital and witness the horrors that have occurred whilst she has been away...

What is it?

Unity based Desktop game demo from the psychological horror genre. Exists three playable chapters of content to explore and discover. All of the artwork and animation is made by mouse and keyboard.

Something very different

This was created as part of my final project whilst at university, this is the last thing I created under the modules. It is also my first horror title, so lots of experimentation within this title!
